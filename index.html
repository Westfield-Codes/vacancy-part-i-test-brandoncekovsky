Job Description

About us

Star Academy for the Gifted and Talented is the place for the brightest students looking for a rigorous curriculum and individual approach. Our dedicated teachers are exceptionally knowledgeable, passionate about their work, and motivated to share their knowledge with your child. We pride ourselves on identifying and nurturing talent in every learner and invite you to be part of our community of learners, parents, caregivers and teachers. Star Academy for the Gifted and Talented is seeking a Computer Science and Robotics Teacher for its Science department.

Responsibilities:

  • Teach Computer Science and Robotics to students.
  • Prepare and deliver rigorous and engaging lesson plans that follow an advanced curriculum.
  • Instruct students in a clear and concise way.
  • Track and record student progress for report cards and parent-teacher conferences.
  • Communicate with parents regarding their child's performance.
  • Provide additional support outside of the classroom for struggling students.
  • Build strong relationships with your students and aid in the strengthening of their self-confidence and motivation while inspiring them to achieve high goals.

Job Skills and Qualifications

  • 3+ years of teaching experience.
  • An advanced degree: Master's, Ph.D, or Ed.D.
  • Experience teaching AP classes
  • Passion for subject/content area.
  • Ability to communicate effectively with students, colleagues and staff.
  • Flexibility and high adaptation skills to work successfully in an unpredictable school setting.
